My Story

Preparation, collaboration, adaptability, and performing at your best as part of a team — the same principles that guide my music have shaped how I lead.

Throughout my career I've been driven by a passion for solving complex business problems — bringing together leadership, stakeholders, designers, and developers to align around a shared vision, then turning that vision into practical digital products, intuitive customer experiences, and scalable technology solutions.

Outside of work, I'm a lifelong drummer and musician who has performed professionally since childhood. I'm also a husband and father of three daughters; one has a rare genetic syndrome, and caring for her has deepened my appreciation for empathy, patience, and what truly matters, in life and in work.
