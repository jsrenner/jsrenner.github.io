My Story

I'm a curious builder, so my career has never fit neatly into one box. It's been shaped by a willingness to learn and a passion for making complicated things work better.

I've always been driven by a passion for solving complex business challenges. I enjoy bringing together leaders, stakeholders, designers, and developers around a shared vision, then turning that vision into practical digital products and intuitive customer experiences. Whether launching a new product, rebranding an organization, modernizing a digital platform, or applying AI to improve workflows, my goal is to create thoughtful, scalable, human-centered solutions that deliver lasting value.

Over the past 26 years, I've led the strategy, design, and development of digital products, platforms, brands, and customer experiences across nonprofit, corporate, startup, and consulting environments. More recently, I've expanded into AI-enabled applications, automations, and business solutions that improve efficiency and help organizations work smarter.

Outside of work, I'm a lifelong drummer and musician. Performing professionally since childhood with touring bands and live productions has reinforced many of the same principles I value professionally: preparation, collaboration, adaptability, and performing at my best under pressure as part of a team.

Most importantly, I'm a husband and father of three daughters. One of my daughters lives with a rare genetic syndrome and significant disabilities. The privilege of caring for her has shaped my leadership, strengthened my resilience, and deepened my appreciation for empathy, patience, and what truly matters in both life and work.
